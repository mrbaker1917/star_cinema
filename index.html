Ticket Policies

We do not pre-sale our tickets. We recommend people to come 30 minutes before their film starts to purchase their tickets.

If you have a group of 6 or more, you can call us (250-655-3384) to reserve seats.

If you are coming in a wheelchair or with someone in a wheelchair, you can call us (250-655-3384) and we can reserve the accessible area for you.
